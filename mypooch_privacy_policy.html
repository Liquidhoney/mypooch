Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 September 2025

We respect your privacy. This page explains what personal data we collect on mypooch.ie, why we collect it, how we handle it, and the rights you have under the GDPR.

Who we are Data we collect How we use data Legal bases (GDPR) Sharing & processors International transfers How long we keep data Your rights Cookies Security Children Changes Contact

1) Who we are

My Pooch (“we”, “us”) operates the website mypooch.ie. We are based in Dublin, Ireland and act as the data controller for the processing described here.

Contact email: info@mypooch.ie

2) Data we collect

  • Contact data — your email address when you join our waitlist or contact us.
  • Message content — any information you include in free-text fields.
  • Technical/operational data — minimal request logs from our hosting (e.g., IP address, user agent, page and timestamp) for security and reliability.

Please avoid sharing sensitive (special-category) data. We do not intentionally collect it.

3) How we use data

  • To run our waitlist and send you updates about our launch and products (only if you opt in).
  • To respond to enquiries and provide customer support.
  • To operate, secure and improve the Site (e.g., troubleshooting, abuse prevention, service analytics based on aggregated logs).
  • To comply with legal obligations (e.g., record-keeping).

4) Legal bases (GDPR)

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) — waitlist/marketing emails.
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) — running a secure website and understanding high-level usage.
  • Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) — responding to messages you send us.
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)) — where applicable.

You can withdraw consent at any time via the unsubscribe link in our emails or by contacting info@mypooch.ie. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of prior processing.

5) Sharing & processors

We do not sell your personal data. We use trusted service providers (“processors”) who process data on our behalf under contracts and appropriate safeguards:

  • Form handling: Formspree — receives form submissions (waitlist/contact).
  • Email hosting: Titan Mail — sends/receives @mypooch.ie email.
  • Website hosting: GitHub Pages — serves our static site.

6) International transfers

Some providers may process data outside the EEA. Where this occurs, we rely on safeguards such as the European Commission’s Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or adequacy decisions as provided by our processors.

7) How long we keep data

  • Waitlist/marketing: until you unsubscribe or we retire the list.
  • Enquiries: typically up to 12 months after the last interaction, unless needed longer to resolve an issue.
  • Operational logs: short, rolling periods unless required to investigate incidents.

8) Your rights

Under GDPR you may have the rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict or object to processing, and to data portability. You can also withdraw consent at any time. You may lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. In Ireland this is the Data Protection Commission.

To exercise your rights, contact info@mypooch.ie. We may need to verify your identity before acting on a request.

9) Cookies

Our Site is a simple static website. We currently do not use third-party tracking cookies. Essential cookies or local storage may be used only to support core functionality (e.g., showing a lightweight success message after submitting a form). If we add analytics or marketing cookies in the future, we will update this notice and request consent where required.

10) Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. If you believe your data may have been compromised, please contact us immediately.

11) Children

Our Site is not directed to children under 16. If you believe a child has provided personal data without appropriate consent, contact us and we will delete it.

12) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be highlighted here with an updated date.

13) Contact

Email us at info@mypooch.ie for any questions or requests about this policy.

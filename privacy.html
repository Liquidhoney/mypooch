Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 September 2025

TL;DR — In short

  • We only collect your email address when you join our waitlist or contact us.
  • We use it only to keep in touch about our launch and products — no spam.
  • Your data is stored securely with trusted providers (Formspree, Titan Mail, GitHub Pages).
  • You can unsubscribe anytime or ask us to delete your data via info@mypooch.ie.

We respect your privacy. This page explains what personal data we collect on mypooch.ie, why we collect it, how we handle it, and the rights you have under the GDPR.

1) Who we are

My Pooch (“we”, “us”) operates the website mypooch.ie. We are based in Dublin, Ireland and act as the data controller for the processing described here.

Contact: info@mypooch.ie

2) Data we collect

  • Contact data — your email address when you join our waitlist or contact us.
  • Message content — information you include in free-text fields.
  • Technical/operational data — minimal request logs (IP, user agent, timestamp) for security and reliability.

Please avoid sharing sensitive (special-category) data. We do not intentionally collect it.

3) How we use data

  • To run our waitlist and send you updates (only if you opt in).
  • To respond to enquiries and provide support.
  • To operate, secure and improve the Site (e.g., troubleshooting, abuse prevention).
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4) Legal bases (GDPR)

  • Consent — waitlist/marketing emails.
  • Legitimate interests — running a secure website.
  • Contract — responding to messages you send us.
  • Legal obligation — where applicable.

You can withdraw consent at any time by contacting info@mypooch.ie.

5) Sharing & processors

We do not sell your data. We use trusted service providers:

  • Form handling: Formspree — receives waitlist/contact submissions.
  • Email hosting: Titan Mail — handles @mypooch.ie email.
  • Website hosting: GitHub Pages — serves our static site.

6) International transfers

Some providers may process data outside the EEA. We rely on safeguards such as SCCs or adequacy decisions.

7) How long we keep data

  • Waitlist/marketing: until you unsubscribe or we retire the list.
  • Enquiries: up to 12 months after last interaction unless needed longer.
  • Logs: short, rolling periods unless needed for incidents.

8) Your rights

Under GDPR you may have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict or object, and to data portability. You may lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Commission.

9) Cookies

Our site is static. We don’t use tracking cookies. Only minimal local storage may be used (e.g., success message after a form).

10) Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data, but no method is 100% secure. If you believe data was compromised, contact us.

11) Children

Our Site is not directed to children under 16. If a child gave us data without consent, contact us to delete it.

12) Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced here.

13) Contact

Email us at info@mypooch.ie for any questions about this policy.

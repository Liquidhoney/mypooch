Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 September 2025

TL;DR — In short

  • We collect your email address and message when you join our waitlist or contact us.
  • We use optional Google Analytics 4 to understand traffic trends — it runs only after you accept analytics cookies.
  • Your data is stored securely with trusted providers (Formspree, Titan Mail, GitHub Pages, Google Ireland Ltd. for analytics if enabled).
  • You can unsubscribe anytime or ask us to delete your data via info@mypooch.ie.

We respect your privacy. This page explains what personal data we collect on mypooch.ie, why we collect it, how we handle it, and the rights you have under the GDPR.

1) Who we are

My Pooch (“we”, “us”) operates the website mypooch.ie. We are based in Dublin, Ireland and act as the data controller for the processing described here.

Contact: info@mypooch.ie

2) Data we collect

  • Contact data — your email address when you join our waitlist or contact us.
  • Message content — information you include in free-text fields.
  • Analytics data (optional) — aggregated usage insights such as pages viewed, approximate location (city/country), device type, and events (e.g., form submissions) collected via Google Analytics 4 only after you opt in to analytics cookies. IP addresses are anonymised.
  • Technical/operational data — minimal request logs (IP, user agent, timestamp) for security and reliability.

Please avoid sharing sensitive (special-category) data. We do not intentionally collect it.

3) How we use data

  • To run our waitlist and send you updates (only if you opt in).
  • To respond to enquiries and provide support.
  • To operate, secure and improve the Site (e.g., troubleshooting, abuse prevention).
  • To understand how people use the Site (analytics) so we can improve content and experience — only after you consent to analytics cookies.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4) Legal bases (GDPR)

  • Consent — waitlist/marketing emails and analytics cookies.
  • Legitimate interests — running a secure website.
  • Contract — responding to messages you send us.
  • Legal obligation — where applicable.

You can withdraw consent at any time by contacting info@mypooch.ie.

5) Sharing & processors

We do not sell your data. We use trusted service providers:

  • Form handling: Formspree — receives waitlist/contact submissions.
  • Email hosting: Titan Mail — handles @mypooch.ie email.
  • Website hosting: GitHub Pages — serves our static site.
  • Analytics (optional): Google Analytics 4 (Google Ireland Ltd.) — provides aggregated usage reports when you consent to analytics cookies.

6) International transfers

Some providers may process data outside the EEA. Formspree and Google Analytics 4 may store data in the United States or other regions. We rely on safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses, adequacy decisions, and Google’s EU data residency controls. Analytics data is pseudonymised and collected only when you give consent.

7) How long we keep data

  • Waitlist/marketing: until you unsubscribe or we retire the list.
  • Enquiries: up to 12 months after last interaction unless needed longer.
  • Logs: short, rolling periods unless needed for incidents.
  • Cookie consent choice: stored for up to 12 months (or until you update it) to remember your preferences.

8) Your rights

Under GDPR you may have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict or object, and to data portability. You may lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Commission.

9) Cookies & consent

We use a small amount of technology to keep the Site functional and to understand what resonates:

  • Essential storage — required to operate the Site, secure our forms, remember that you closed the cookie banner, and store your cookie preference. This uses first-party local storage or a lightweight cookie.
  • Analytics cookies (optional) — Google Analytics 4 sets first-party cookies only after you consent. They measure visits, pages viewed, approximate location (city/country), device information, and events such as waitlist sign-ups. IP addresses are anonymised and advertising features are disabled.

On your first visit we ask for consent through our banner. Analytics stays disabled until you click “Accept”. If you choose “Decline”, we respect that decision.

You can change your mind anytime via the link in the footer. When you withdraw consent we stop sending new analytics data and update Google Consent Mode to disable analytics storage.

10) Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data, but no method is 100% secure. If you believe data was compromised, contact us.

11) Children

Our Site is not directed to children under 16. If a child gave us data without consent, contact us to delete it.

12) Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced here.

13) Contact

Email us at info@mypooch.ie for any questions about this policy.
